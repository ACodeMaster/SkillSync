Frequently Asked Questions

What is SkillSync?
SkillSync is an AI-powered tool that analyzes your resume against a job description to show you how well you match the required skills.
How does the analysis work?
Our tool scans your uploaded resume and compares keywords to the skills listed in the job description, providing a percentage match and highlighting matched and missing skills.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Your uploaded files are processed temporarily and deleted immediately after analysis. We do not store or share your personal data.
What file formats are supported?
SkillSync currently supports PDF and DOCX for resumes and plain text (.txt) for job descriptions.
